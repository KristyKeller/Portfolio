Detail-oriented Full-Stack Web Developer with a passion for ceaseless learning and building dynamic web and mobile applications. I have been in the eCommerce industry for nearly three years and adore the problem solving environment the technology field fosters, leading me to pursue a path further involved with back-end and front-end development.

I have a Full Stack Web Development certificate from the University of Pennsylvania. I have experience as a freelance Content Marketer along with being a eCommerce Content Specialist at Dick's Sporting Goods.

From design to execution, I utilize creativity and technical skills to provide seamless end to end projects and results through JavaScript, Reactjs, React Native, jQuery, Bootstrap, and other libraries and frameworks. Check out my portfolio for a better look at my way of thinking.
